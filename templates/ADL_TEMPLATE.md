# Architectural Decision Log (ADL) — One Decision

A lightweight, one-page record for capturing architectural decisions clearly and explicitly.

**One ADL = one decision.**

---

## Decision Title
Short, descriptive name (one line).

**Decision ID**  
ADL-YYYYMMDD-XXX

**Date**  
YYYY-MM-DD

**Owner**  
Name and role — the person accountable for this decision.

**Status**  
Proposed | Decided | Superseded | Reversed | Deferred

---

## Context
Concise description of the system context, business need, constraints, and relevant background.
Limit to what materially affects the decision.

---

## Options Considered
- **Option A** — one-line summary  
- **Option B** — one-line summary  
- **Option C** — one-line summary  

---

## Decision
Statement of what was decided (one sentence).

---

## Rationale
Why this option was chosen over the alternatives.
Capture trade-offs, constraints, and risks consciously accepted.

---

## Impact
- **Systems affected** — services, modules, teams  
- **Operational impact** — monitoring, SLOs, runbooks  
- **Security / privacy impact** — yes / no; brief note if yes  
- **Estimated reversal cost** — e.g. low / 1–2 sprints / DB migration required  

---

## Required Actions
- Action — Owner — Due date  
- Action — Owner — Due date  

---

## Dependencies & Inputs
Upstream decisions, approvals, data, or events required for this decision to be implemented.

---

## Review / Revisit Criteria
When this decision should be reviewed or reconsidered  
(e.g. after 6 months; if latency > X ms; if traffic > Y rps).

---

## Change Log
- YYYY-MM-DD — Owner — status change or note
